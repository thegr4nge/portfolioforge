    9. **ato_example_12_sonya** — Single DisposedLot: ticker="TLP", acquired=date(2023,1,15), disposed=date(2023,10,20), cost_basis_aud=1500.0, proceeds_aud=2300.0, gain_aud=800.0, discount_applied=False. marginal_tax_rate=0.45. Result: 1 TaxYearResult, cgt_payable=360.0 (800 × 0.45, no discount).
    10. **ato_example_16_mei_ling** — Single DisposedLot: gain_aud=8000.0, discount_applied=True. Prior losses: pass a DisposedLot with gain_aud=-1000.0, discount_applied=False (same year). Build: net non-discountable = 0 (loss fully absorbs short-term gains), remaining losses = 0, net_discount = 8000 - 1000 = 7000, discounted = 3500. marginal_tax_rate=0.32. cgt_payable = 3500 × 0.32 = 1120.
    11. **loss_offsets_non_discount_gains_first** — Two lots same year: lot A (gain=500, discount_applied=False), lot B (loss=-300, discount_applied=False), lot C (gain=1000, discount_applied=True). Expected: net non-discount = max(0, 500-300)=200; remaining losses=0; net_discount = 1000; discounted=500. net_cgt = 200 + 500 = 700.
    12. **no_discount_on_loss** — DisposedLot with gain_aud=-500.0, discount_applied=False. cgt_payable=0.0 (losses do not generate a credit in this model — net_cgt cannot go below 0).
    13. **multiple_tax_years** — Three DisposedLots across two tax years (FY2024 and FY2025). build_tax_year_results() returns 2 TaxYearResult items, one per year, correctly sorted ascending by ending_year.

    Implementation note:
    - `build_tax_year_results()` signature: `(disposed_lots: list[DisposedLot], marginal_tax_rate: float) -> list[TaxYearResult]`
    - After-tax return in TaxYearResult: `after_tax_return = sum(lot.gain_aud for lot in year_lots) - cgt_payable` (gross gain minus tax owed; simple for now)
    - No `dividend_income` or `franking_credits_claimed` in this function — those come from franking.py; TaxYearResult fields are zero-filled here, updated by franking engine later
    - `cgt_events` = number of DisposedLots in the year
    - The loss-ordering algorithm from RESEARCH.md Pitfall 1 is the definitive implementation:
      1. net_non_discount = max(0, sum of non-discount gains - total losses)
      2. remaining_losses = max(0, total_losses - sum of non-discount gains)
      3. net_discount = max(0, sum of discount gains - remaining_losses)
      4. discounted = net_discount * 0.5
      5. net_cgt = net_non_discount + discounted
      6. cgt_payable = net_cgt * marginal_tax_rate
  </behavior>
