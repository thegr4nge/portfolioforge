Manual / Excel PortfolioForge

45-day rule Often missed or partially applied. Small-shareholder exemption incorrectly assumed to apply. Always enforced. $5,000 exemption not applied — does not exist for SMSFs.ITAA 1936 s.160APHO
Loss ordering Capital losses applied manually, often against discountable gains first — the wrong order. ATO ordering enforced: losses net against non-discountable gains first, then discountable.ITAA 1997 s.102-15

Division 296. Know your exposure before the ATO does.

An additional 15% tax on superannuation earnings for members whose Total Super Balance exceeds $3 million. Legislated November 2024. PortfolioForge models your liability year by year — and shows exactly how contributions strategy and pension drawdowns change the number.

ATO formula
Exact calculation, not an estimate
Hard-wires the ATO formula: super_earnings = TSB_end + benefits_paid − TSB_start − NCC. Concessional contributions reduce the effective threshold. Non-concessional contributions are excluded from earnings but raise the taxable proportion — the gotcha in most DIY models.
Planning scenarios
Three strategies, side by side
Every projection runs three automatic scenarios: status quo, stop concessional contributions, and accelerated pension drawdown. Quantifies the saving from each strategy in dollar terms — not qualitative advice, actual numbers.
SMSF trustees
The cash-flow problem for illiquid assets
Unrealised gains on property and unlisted assets flow through to the TSB — and therefore into the Division 296 calculation. The tax liability exists even if the asset has not been sold. PortfolioForge flags years where the tax liability is material relative to liquid holdings.
