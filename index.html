What PortfolioForge gets right

The rules that matter most for SMSF CGT are the ones most tools handle incorrectly.

SMSF CGT discount: 33.33%

Not 50%. Under ATO s.115-100, SMSFs in accumulation phase receive a one-third discount, not the individual rate. PortfolioForge applies this correctly by default.

Anniversary date logic

The 12-month threshold uses the legal anniversary date, not 365 days. Disposing on the exact anniversary date does NOT qualify for the discount. Leap years are handled correctly.

ATO loss-ordering rule

Capital losses are netted against non-discountable gains first, then discountable gains, before the discount is applied. Most spreadsheets apply the discount before netting losses.

45-day franking rule

Always enforced for SMSFs. The $5,000 small-shareholder exemption does not apply to superannuation funds. The rule is checked at the correct ex-dividend date.

Cross-year loss carry-forward

Net capital losses carry forward indefinitely under Australian tax law. PortfolioForge tracks the running balance across all financial years in the analysis period.

FIFO with partial lot splitting

A sell order that consumes multiple acquisition parcels is handled correctly. Partial lots are split at the exact share count, with the remainder carried forward.
