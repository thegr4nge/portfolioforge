# Demo Guide
*For use on a 15-minute discovery call. Follow in order.*

---

## Before the call

Download the example report from the landing page so you can share your screen
and show it without needing to run anything live:
https://portfolioforge-au.netlify.app/

Also open the live demo in a tab:
https://portfolioforge-vupb82qsizvlzjte4jilg4.streamlit.app/

---

## Opening (30 seconds — say this verbatim)

> "Thanks for your time. PortfolioForge is a tool I built for SMSF accountants.
> It runs ATO-correct CGT calculations and exports a Word document you can
> attach to a client file. Important: PortfolioForge is a tool for you to use
> with clients — we don't provide financial advice to end investors.
> Three things in five minutes."

---

## Step 1 — Show the output first (2 minutes)

Open the example Word report (`Meridian_Family_SMSF_CGT_Report.docx`).

Show in this order:

1. **CGT event log** — acquired date, disposed date, cost base, proceeds,
   net gain, 33.33% SMSF discount applied, ATO rule annotation.
   Say: *"Every disposal traced to the specific ATO rule. Your auditor can
   follow every number independently."*

2. **Year-by-year tax table** — FY, CGT events, CGT payable, franking credits,
   carry-forward loss balance.
   Say: *"The carry-forward loss column is the one most manual calculations
   get wrong — losses net against non-discountable gains first, before
   discountable gains, before the discount applies. PortfolioForge
   implements that correctly."*

3. **Key numbers** — total return, after-tax CAGR, CGT payable.
   Say: *"This is what the client actually took home after tax."*

---

## Step 2 — Show the live demo (2 minutes)

Open https://portfolioforge-vupb82qsizvlzjte4jilg4.streamlit.app/

broker CSV upload.

type, and clicks run. The Word document comes out the other side. Under 10
seconds."*
